Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2025

Welcome to Smart Kids Quiz! We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our young users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our educational quiz application.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Kids Quiz is designed to be safe and educational for children. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information from users.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as quiz scores, subject preferences, and feature interactions to improve the app experience.
  • Local Storage: Progress and achievements are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. Data Usage

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences and track progress locally
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Generate AI-powered explanations and educational content
  • Display relevant educational videos from YouTube

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Smart Kids Quiz uses minimal cookies and local storage technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic app functionality, such as remembering language preferences and quiz progress.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the app to improve our services. These are anonymized and do not identify individual users.
  • No Third-Party Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Smart Kids Quiz is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Age-Appropriate Content: All content is curated to be educational and safe for children aged 4-14.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • No Registration Required: Children can use the app without creating accounts or providing personal details.
  • Parental Control: Parents have full visibility into app activities as all data is stored locally on their devices.
  • Third-Party Services: Embedded YouTube videos comply with YouTube's Terms of Service and are shown in a controlled environment.

5. Third-Party Services

Smart Kids Quiz integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Used to generate AI-powered explanations and educational content. No personal information is sent to OpenAI.
  • YouTube: Educational videos are embedded from YouTube. YouTube's privacy policy applies to embedded content.
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser types) for security and performance purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage of API keys and sensitive information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No storage of payment or financial information (the app is free)

7. User Rights

Users and parents have the right to:

  • Access and review locally stored data at any time
  • Delete all local data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of optional features such as voice recognition
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or questions

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will indicate when the policy was last revised. Continued use of Smart Kids Quiz after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
